# Municipality Econ Data Stat Austria

Municipality level data from StatAustria:
<https://www.statistik.at/web_de/statistiken/wirtschaft/oeffentliche_finanzen_und_steuern/oeffentliche_finanzen/gebarungen_der_oeffentlichen_rechtstraeger/index.html>

Subscription for 14 days (100.000 values) at Euro 50.- only communal tax
2011-2019 or only 2019 or Euro 150,- for several variables
<https://www.statistik.at/web_en/forms/publications_services/database_sdb/index.html>
Budget from AMS or Max research budget

Stat Austria provides GKZ for each municipality. 2000 - 2020

## Needed

-   Kommunalsteuer [ABO]

## Not needed

-   Stand an öffentlich Bediensteten ((full-time
    equivalent) [ABO]
-   Personalaufw: gesamt [ABO]
-   Invest: gesamt [ABO]
-  LT-Ausgaben: gesamt [ABO]
-  KT-Ausgaben: gesamt [ABO]
-  LT-Einnahmen: gesamt [ABO]
-  KT-Einnahmen: gesamt [ABO]


## Available by subscription:

-   Communal tax [ABO]
-   Labour costs in total [ABO]
-   Investments in total [ABO]
-   Number of persons employed in the public sector ((full-time
    equivalent) [ABO]

## Free:

-   Debts final result in total
-   Ordinary expenditures in total
-   Ordinary revenuess in total
-   Inhabitants
